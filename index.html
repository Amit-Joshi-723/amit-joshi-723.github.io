GenAI Product Analytics Framework
● Live
What to measure when the product itself is AI

Most GenAI products are measured like web apps — sessions, pageviews, time on site. This framework argues for something different. Loyal users who fill in surveys are telling you exactly what your dashboard cannot see. Connect their verbatims to your zero metrics. Measure output quality, not engagement. Instrument before you build, not after something goes wrong.

Output Acceptance Rate and Prompt Refinement Rate as primary health metrics — not session count
LLM-powered verbatim analysis connected to metric anomalies
